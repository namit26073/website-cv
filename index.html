About

Hello and welcome to my website. I am currently a second year student of MEng Design Engineering at Imperial College London. Get to know about me by browsing through this website.

Intro

As a design engineer, I have always been interested with the inter-relation of creativity and technology and how they can be fused together to solve complex problems in easy ways.

My Inspiration / Motivation

My natural inclination of taking things apart and trying my best to assemble them back together has always been there, whether this was my personal laptop or a crooked IKEA chair. However, my motivation was augmented when I realised the lack of innovation for disabled people, while I was a wheelchair user myself. This forced me to concieve the idea to make my first ever product: a portable wheelchair ramp.

Since then I have broadened my interests within engineering and ventured into multiple projects each of which has contributed to add something new to my skillsets. You can browse through some of my projects in portfolio section.

My Interests

Outside of Engineering, my interests lie in playing sports such as badminton, basketball and bouldering. I also enjoy playing guitar from time to time and I currently have a devoloping passion towards motorcycles.
